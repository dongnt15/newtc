MILESTONES

NEWTECONS Investment Construction Joint Stock Company was established on October 23rd 2003, it was known to be one of the prestige companies in construction sector with the various types of projects.

2003

Established F.D.C. Real Estate Investment Construction Joint Stock Company.

2009

Participated in the construction of the premium apartment complex – Indochina Plaza Hanoi commercial and service center, which won 5 honorable regional awards for interior design and architecture.

2011

Established Waterproofing division, specializing in constructing and providing waterproofing products and solutions for constructions. Waterproofing division, a part of F.D.C, is the partner of multiple major contractors across the country.

2012

Marked a significant milestone of F.D.C when investors selected F.D.C as the General Contractor for key projects such as Phu My 3, Texhong Ngan Long Factory, Jotun Factory, etc.

2014

Established Aluminium and Glass division, affirming the brand through many major projects: Vinhomes Central Park III, Regina Hai Phong factory, Big C Ha Long, etc.

2015

Participated in the construction of CT5 Masteri Thao Dien tower, reaching project sales of 1,000 billion.

2016

Participated in the construction of the “meta-project” The Landmark 81 - the tallest building in Vietnam. F.D.C was responsible for the elevator core and steel structure, which required the highest expert techniques of all The Landmark 81 project.

2017

Listed in Top 10 Prestigious construction contractors and Top 500 Fastest growing enterprises in Vietnam.

2018

Continued to be listed in Top 10 Prestigious construction contractors and Top 500 Fastest growing enterprises in Vietnam; received a Certificate of Merit from the Ministry of Labor, War Invalids & Social Welfare for achievements in occupational safety and health for 3 consecutive years.

2019

Changed name to NEWTECONS Construction Investment Joint Stock Company.

2020

Honored in Top 50 Fastest growing enterprises in Vietnam.

2021

Listed in Top 10 Prestigious construction contractors and Top 50 Fastest growing enterprises in Vietnam. Newtecons inaugurated the Newtecons Tower, marking a proud milestone in the process of expansion across the globe.
