NEWTECONS OPENING REPRESENTATIVE OFFICE IN HANOI

[Hanoi, January 6, 2023] Newtecons opened a representative office at the address LK- C29, Embassy Garden villa, Hoang Minh Thao, Xuan Tao ward, Bac Tu Liem district, Hanoi. This is the “second house” to create cohesion, helping Newtecons gradually realize the strategy of strongly developing projects in the North.

Moving the representative office to the same location with companies in the Ecosystem such as Ricons, SOL E&C, Boho is a way for Newtecons to combine resources and increase strength. It’s believed that the Northern field will be strongly developed and contribute to affirming Newtecons’ position in the market.
