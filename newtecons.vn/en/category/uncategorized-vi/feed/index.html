]]> The Union Board of Newtecons recently visited Ba Tang commune, Huong Hoa district, Quang Tri province, as part of a series of yearly charitable endeavors, and presented gifts to the locals. This event was funded by Newtercons people who participated in the online Newtecons Running Challenge 2022; for each kilometer completed, 1,000 VND will be donated to the “coats of love” program.

On this trip, Newtecons visited poor locals and gave them numerous practical gifts. Warm clothes were presented to children aged 3 to 5, as well as school bags and notebooks to the top performance pupils of primary school. Milk and sweets for children, as well as practical gifts for the poor locals, totaling 110 million.

The People’s Committee representative of Ba Tang Commune, Mr. Nguyen Huu Khoa, shared: “Ba Tang is the most isolated commune of Huong Hoa district. This area is very far from the district center, so there are few sponsors come here for charity, the rate of poor households accounts for 68.75%; people live dispersed on the high hillside; in the winter, children do not have warm clothes; it is very cold; in addition to crop failure, children are severely malnourished. People of Ba Floor commune are very appreciative that Newtecons company has come here twice to share and help the locals. On behalf of local government, I would like to thank you for providing a beneficial and useful program to assist the locals. Thanks for your kindness and care, and I look forward to receiving more learning materials and goodwill from your company”.

The principal of Ba Tang Kindergarten, Ms. Nguyen Thi Diem Ngoc, spoke on behalf of the 377 kids who were given gifts this time, shared: “Today is a wonderful day because the Coats of love program is bringing warm clothes and gifts to the kids in a poor commune, Ba Tang. The locals here are very poor, thus kids lack a lot, especially in terms of personal items that safeguard kids’ health. The school always makes an effort to provide excellent care and education for our children, but it lacks the resources to provide further support for them, we really need greater engagement from the sponsors to enhance their living and learning environments. Your program supports warm clothing while also spreading love and care.

Ba Tang is a border commune located 50 kilometers south of Huong Hoa district, on the Vietnam-Laos border. Despite its many challenges, Ba Tang commune strives every day to mobilize internal and external resources to develop the economy and improve the lives of its residents, particularly children.
