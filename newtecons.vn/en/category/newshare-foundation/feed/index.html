The post NEWTECONS CONGRATULATED ON THE INTERNATIONAL WOMEN’S DAY ON 08/03/2021 appeared first on Newtecons.

]]> On 6 March 2021, under the agreement of the BOM, Union Executive Board of Newtecons organized a meeting to congratulate on International Women’s Day on 8 March 2021. The meeting has brought many surprises and joys to 40 female staff of Newtecons who are working in departments and construction sites throughout the country.

In the meeting, the female staff gathered, shared and re-watched the interesting videos made by the male colleagues in the company. As in every year, this year’s meeting had the attendance of Mr. Nguyen Quang Thuy – CEO together with the male managers representing “men” of Newtecons. In here, Mr. Anh Thuy sent sincere acknowledgements to the great efforts of female staff at Newtecons in excellent completion of tasks, resourceful and whole in the family business. He together with male staff participated in giving meaningful gifts and beautiful bunches of flowers to female staff.

For a company operating in construction filed as Newtecons, female staff just account for 10% of the number but play an important role in the overall success. Meaningful activities on International Women’s Day on 8/3 at Newtecons company has shown the deep care of the Executive Board. Hopefully, female staff of Newtecons in particular and all of company’s employees in general will keep the faith in making contribution and regard Newtecons company as their second home.

]]> (HN – July 28, 2019) Newtecons Championship 2019 – the Northern Region has successfully closed, after more than 12 hours of competition from 9 teams of Newtecons, Coteccons Group and partner units. The tournament was attended by representatives of the Board of Management of the Company, Mr. Nguyen Phan Le Tuong – Project Director and all employees working in the North Region.

At the program, Mr. Nguyen Phan Le Tuong shared this tournament was organized to create an environment of cohesion and exchange between Newtecons, Coteccons Group and partners. Moreover, Mr. Tuong hopes this useful sport activity will be maintained and invested more by the Trade Union of the Company.

Accordingly, the results of the tournament are recorded as follows:

The Champion: Coalition of Vin Gia Lam Subcontractors

Runner-up: Vincity Tay Mo Site Management Team

Third Place: Yen Phat Subcontractor

Consolation Prize: Berriver Site Management Team

Best player: Nguyen Huu Tho – Equipment Department of Newtecons

Top scorer: Nguyen Quang Huy – Director of Yen Phat Subcontractor

In particular, Organization board and Board of Management also donated more prizes for the tournament:

Gold glove: Le Huy Huan – Vincity Tay Mo Site Management Team

Outstanding supporters: Vincity Gia Lam Site Management Team

Guest prize: Hanoi Office
